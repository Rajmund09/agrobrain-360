Platform Overview

Everything a farmer needs, in one offline AI app.

AgroBrain 360 is an offline-first AI platform designed for the 118 million farmers in India who rely on basic smartphones and often lack reliable internet connectivity.

By running advanced machine learning models directly on-device, the platform delivers real-time agricultural intelligence without requiring a data connection.
